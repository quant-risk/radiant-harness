# Vision — Pulse

## Elevator pitch
For **product teams** that need **frictionless in-app feedback**, **Pulse** is an
**embeddable widget** that **collects contextual feedback at the moment of use**.

## The product IS / IS NOT
| IS                         | IS NOT                    |
|----------------------------|---------------------------|
| lightweight embeddable widget | analytics suite       |
| contextual collection      | moderation tool          |

## Personas
- **PM** — wants qualitative signal without setting up a survey.

## North Star
- Contextual feedbacks collected per week. Baseline **0** → target **200** in pilot.
