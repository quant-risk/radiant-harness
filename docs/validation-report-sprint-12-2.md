## 7. Decisions

- ✅ Sprint 12 second batch is **READY TO MERGE** at v0.4.5.
- ✅ The read-only guarantee is the right design choice — auto-
  configuring MCPs without explicit approval is a data-leak risk
  (per the integracoes skill's account-boundary gate). Adding/
  removing MCPs is a project-level decision that belongs in the
  approval interview, not in a CLI auto-flag.
- ✅ `radiant init --all` does NOT auto-create `.mcp.json`. Users
  must explicitly invoke the integracoes skill or hand-write the
  file. This is by design.
